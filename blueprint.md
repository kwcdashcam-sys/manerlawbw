# App Blueprint: Maner Law - Professional Legal Solutions

## Core Features:

- **Firm Overview:** A comprehensive overview of Maner Law's history, mission, and values. This will be the main landing page, establishing the firm's brand and expertise.

- **Practice Areas:** A detailed section or page showcasing the specific areas of law the firm specializes in. Each area will have a summary of services offered.

- **Attorney Profiles:** Individual profiles for each attorney, featuring their biography, areas of expertise, and professional background.

- **Contact Page:** A dedicated page with a user-friendly contact form for inquiries, along with the firm's physical address, phone number, email, and an interactive map.

## Style Guidelines:

- **Primary Color:** A professional and deep shade of blue (`#1a365d`) to convey trust, stability, and authority.
- **Background Color:** A very light, almost-white blue (`#F0F9FF`) to provide a clean and airy feel, derived from a desaturated tint of the primary color.
- **Accent Color:** A subtle and elegant gold (`#A8915F`) to be used for highlights, calls-to-action, and key visual elements.
- **Typography:**
  - **Headlines & Body:** 'Inter', a clean and modern sans-serif font that is highly readable and professional.
- **Iconography:** Simple, clean, and professional icons to visually represent practice areas and other key information.
- **Layout:** A structured, clean, and modern layout with clear calls-to-action to guide users through the site.
- **Imagery:** High-quality, professional images that reflect the firm's culture and values. Placeholder images will be used initially.
