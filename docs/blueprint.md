# **App Name**: Manrel Law - Professional Legal Solutions

## Core Features:

- Firm Overview: Display a comprehensive overview of Manrel Law's history, mission, and values.
- Practice Areas: Detail the specific areas of law in which Manrel Law specializes, with summaries of typical services offered.
- Attorney Profiles: Showcase individual attorney profiles, including biographies, expertise, and contact information.
- Contact Form: Provide a contact page with a detailed inquiry form and firm location.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Dark blue (#1E3A8A) to convey trust and professionalism.
- Background color: Very light blue (#F0F9FF), a heavily desaturated tint of the primary hue.
- Accent color: Subtle gold (#A8915F) for highlights and accents, providing a touch of elegance.
- Body and headline font: 'Literata', a serif font providing a literary and professional feel.
- Use simple, professional icons to represent each area of expertise.
- Maintain a clean, structured layout with clear calls to action to enhance user experience.
- Use subtle transitions and animations to enhance engagement without being distracting.
